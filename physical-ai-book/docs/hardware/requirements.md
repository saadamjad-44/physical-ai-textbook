# Hardware Requirements

Physical AI is computationally demanding. It involves Physics Simulation, Visual Perception, and Generative AI.

## The Digital Twin Workstation (Required)

NVIDIA Isaac Sim requires RTX (Ray Tracing) capabilities. Standard laptops will not work.

### Recommended Specs:
- **GPU**: NVIDIA RTX 4070 Ti (12GB VRAM) or higher.
- **CPU**: Intel Core i7 (13th Gen+) or AMD Ryzen 9.
- **RAM**: 64 GB DDR5 (32 GB minimum).
- **OS**: Ubuntu 22.04 LTS (Native or Dual Boot).

## The Physical AI Edge Kit

For physical deployment, we recommend:
- **Brain**: NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano (8GB) or Orin NX (16GB).
- **Eyes**: Intel RealSense D435i or D455 (with IMU).
- **Voice**: USB Microphone/Speaker array.

## Robot Options
- **Budget**: Unitree Go2 Edu (~$3,000) - Quadruped proxy.
- **Intermediate**: Unitree G1 (~$16,000) - Miniature Humanoid.
- **Premium**: Unitree H1 ($90k+).
